1. Description of Service

2. User Responsibilities

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026.
This Privacy Policy explains how Zenless Showcase Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service to display Zenless Zone Zero (ZZZ) statistics on your Discord profile.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the /register command and link your HoYoverse account, we collect and store the following information:

  • Your Discord User ID.
  • Your Zenless Zone Zero (ZZZ) UID.
  • Your HoYoverse session cookies, specifically your ltoken_v2 and ltuid_v2 values.
  • Your preferences for which in-game agents you wish to display in your widget slots.

2. How We Use Your Information

The Bot uses your provided data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate with HoYoverse APIs on your behalf to fetch your ZZZ game records, including your account nickname, level, server name, avatar, and unlocked agents.
  • To format these game records and push them to your Discord profile widget using Discord's API.

3. Data Security and Storage

Protecting your HoYoverse account information is a priority:

  • Your data is stored in a MongoDB database.
  • Your HoYoverse ltoken_v2 and ltuid_v2 cookies are never stored in plaintext within the database.
  • Before being stored, these cookies are symmetrically encrypted using Fernet (AES-128-CBC + HMAC-SHA256).
  • Plaintext cookies are only ever held temporarily in memory during the active process of fetching your game data.

4. Data Deletion and Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can opt out of the service at any time by using the /unregister command.
  • Executing this command will immediately and permanently delete your user configuration, UID, and encrypted cookies from our database.
  • Once unregistered, your account will no longer be picked up by automated widget updates.

5. Third-Party Services

To function, the Bot transmits data to two external services:

  • HoYoverse: Your UID and encrypted cookies are decrypted in memory to request your ZZZ game data from HoYoverse servers.
  • Discord: Your fetched game data is sent to Discord's API to dynamically update the fields on your user profile identity.
